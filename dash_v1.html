How the Four Pillars continuously improve Multi-Agentic Apps

Each pillar feeds the next in a continuous quality loop. Correctness evaluators surface where agents fail; Reliability tooling ensures they recover deterministically; Observability traces reveal exactly why they failed; Governance gates prevent bad patterns re-entering production — making every release measurably more trustworthy than the last.

✅
Correctness → sets the quality bar
LLM-as-judge evaluators score KYC, AML, PEP, and escalation decisions. CI blocks regressions before they ship.
🔁
Reliability → makes quality durable
Temporal replays ensure the correct execution path is retried after any infra failure — not just on the happy path.
🔭
Observability → makes quality visible
Every span carries eval annotations. Regressions appear as score drops on Phoenix before users are ever affected.
⚖️
Governance → keeps quality auditable
Four-layer audit stack + cost gates ensure every AI decision is explainable to regulators and engineering teams.
